Get to know us better

Sara Antunes is a reseracher at CIIMAR and a professor at Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade do Porto. Sara's research has been focus on ecology and aquatic ecotoxicology with high scientific expression, through many scientific papers and books published. Sara's attention is more dragged into the field of environmental monitoring, mainly about the aquatic ecossistem , through water auality asssessment.

Rita has a Bachelor in Biology and master in Cellular and Molecular Biology, by the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade do Porto. Rita has been focus her work in production of compounds by Planctomycetes and its potential aplication in biotechnology. Rita is a PhD student at CIIMAR.

Conceição has a Bachelor in Biology and master in Ecology, by the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade do Porto. Conceição studied alternatives and supplementar sources of food for Daphnia magna, aiming to reduce the depency on just one food source. Nowadays, is a PhD student in CIIMAR, studing the Planctomycetes use as a food source for many aquatic organisms.

Olga Maria Lage is a professor in the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade do Porto and principal investigator of a group from CIIMAR - Centro Interdisciplinar de Investigação Marinha e Ambiental. Olga's main field of interest is Planctomycetes, a small group of bacteria , and studies all the aspects of this microorganisms : from isolation, to characterization of new taxa , genomics and its potential aplication in biotechnology.
